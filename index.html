Projects

Swornfish

Winner of the EECS494 Winter 2025 Games showcase!

I led the design and implementation of the final worm boss and the anglerfish level, and developed core UI systems including health displays and toast notifications. I worked closely with a team of four developers to polish gameplay and resolve bugs throughout the project. Additionally, I created and edited the official game trailer, incorporating team feedback to refine its pacing, visuals, and messaging. We used Unity, C#, and Jira for development and project management.

Stepforge

A small 2D platformer game where players must carefully place blocks beneath them as they traverse the 10 different levels.

I led the complete design and development of the game, handling all aspects of production from concept to release. Technologies used include Unity and C#.

1986 The Legend of Zelda Unity Clone

A clone of the 1986 NES The Legend of Zelda game featuring the first dungeon and a complete custom level with a special boss fight.

I was responsible for approximately 50% of the overall design, coding, and creative direction for this project. The remaining contributions were handled by my partner, with both of us sharing tasks and decisions equally throughout the process. Technologies used include Unity and C#.
